Help the Padawans gather crystals!

The new class of Jedi Padawans is hunting for their kyber crystals, and they need your help!

Hunt for crystals by hitting the number targets and collecting crystals to construct their lightsabers.

    How To Play
  • -You'll be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • -There are four crystals below; by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score
  • -You win the game by matching your total to the random number, and lose if you go over.
  • -The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.
